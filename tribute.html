Cristiano Ronaldo

Mr Champions League

Ronaldo is widely regarded as one of the two best players of his generation, alongside Lionel Messi.

Club career:

  • Sporting CP - on 29 September 2002, Ronaldo made his debut in the Primeira Liga.
  • Manchester United - Ronaldo became United's first Ballon d'Or winner since Best in 1968.
  • Real Madrid - Ronaldo had scored 252 goals in 228 matches in La Liga to become the competition's second-highest goalscorer.
  • Juventus - Ronaldo ended the season with 29 league goals, winning the Capocannoniere award.
  • Return to Manchester United - The first 24 hours of Ronaldo's shirt sales was reported to have broken the all-time record following a transfer.
  • Al Nassr - Ronaldo received the highest football salary in history, worth €200 million per year.
